We Scare Because We Care

Mike Wazowski

Michael Wazowski also known as Mike. He is a lime green spherical monster with one large green eye, two small horns on his head, and thin arms and legs attached to either side of his torso.

Randall Boggs

Randall "Randy" Boggs is a chameleon/gecko-like monster that was a member of the Roar Omega Roar fraternity at Monsters University and worked as a scarer for Monsters, Inc..

James P. Sullivan

James "Jimmy" P. "Sulley" Sullivan is a huge monster with shaggy aqua fur with purple blotches, two small horns on his head, and a long tail.
